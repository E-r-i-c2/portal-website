Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click the "Log In" button in the top right and select "Sign Up" to create your free Portal account.
I forgot my password. What should I do?
On the login modal, click "Forgot password?" and follow the instructions to reset your password.
How do I report a bug or issue?
Please use the Support page to contact us or email support@portal.com with details about the issue.
Can I suggest a new game or feature?
Absolutely! We welcome suggestions. Use the Support page or our feedback form to share your ideas.
Where can I find the latest updates?
Check the News page for the latest updates, announcements, and new features.
